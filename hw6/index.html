Our Mission

The Campus QR is a campus web application that allows clubs, classes, and student groups to organize events and track attendance digitally.

Organizers create events by specifying title, type, description, date/time, location, and capacity. Once created, the system generates QR‑coded tickets. A dashboard lets organizers see real‑time counts of registered, checked‑in, and remaining seats.

Attendees register for events and receive a unique QR code ticket. On arrival, attendees present their ticket, which is scanned to mark check‑in. This ensures quick entry and prevents duplicate or invalid scans.

Overall, the Campus QR eliminates manual sign‑in sheets, ensures fair capacity management, and provides both organizers and attendees with a streamlined, reliable event experience.
